Boeing Internship 2019 Virtual Card Sorting Tool

Context :A team was in the preliminary stages of designing a website for the Enterprise and wanted to determine if the sitemap of the website was sufficient and user-friendly. To perform user testing,the team conducted in-person virtual card sorting (VCS) of the sitemap of services for teams located in the Charleston area.
Problem
Proposed Solution I co-designed a Virtual Card Sorting (VCS) Tool that allowed my team to virtually collect end-user feedback on a website that our team was developing. The VCS Tool was iteratively designed and hosted in the Excel Microsoft Application. This tool was valuable for my team because it allowed end-users from across the enterprise to interactively give diverse website feeedback through virtual means.
